"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication"  -Leonardo da Vinci

Steere Engineering Incorporated is a civil engineering firm offering bridge, contractor support, structural, highway, traffic, environmental permitting, NBIS bridge inspection, and 3D CAD modeling and rendering services. Steere takes a simple, yet innovative approach to complex challenges combining our commitment to quality, our integrity, and many years of experience in a wide range of engineering endeavors to deliver projects that are responsive to our client’s needs as well as budget and schedule. DBE/WBE Certified Rhode Island, Massachusetts, and Connecticut.
